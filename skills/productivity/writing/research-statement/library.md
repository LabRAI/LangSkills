# Library

## Outline template (copy-paste)

```text
Title / Research Vision (1–2 sentences)

Thrust 1: <name>
- Big question & why it matters:
- Approach / methods:
- Evidence from prior work:
- Milestones (1/3/5 years):
- Risks & alternatives:

Thrust 2: <name>
...

Thrust 3: <optional>
...

Funding & broader impacts (optional but recommended)
- Likely programs / agencies:
- Outreach / education / dissemination:

Fit
- Why this department:
- Potential collaborations / resources:

Closing (3–5 bullets)
```

## Quick checklist

```text
- Clear 1-sentence vision in the first paragraph
- 2–3 thrusts, each with feasibility + milestones
- Independence and niche stated explicitly
- Fit paragraph is specific (names/resources/centers)
- Readable to non-specialists; minimal jargon
- Within requested length/format
```

## Prompt snippet

```text
You are an academic writing assistant. Help draft or revise a faculty research statement.

Inputs:
- Job ad + department context
- My 2–3 research thrusts and 3 representative papers
- Constraints: length, audience, and whether to include funding/broader impacts

Output:
- A structured outline + a revision checklist
- 3–5 bullet closing summary

Rules:
- Avoid generic claims; every claim must be supported by evidence or a concrete plan.
- Optimize for readability by mixed-discipline committees.
```
