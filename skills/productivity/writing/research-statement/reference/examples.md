# Examples

## 30 秒摘要（写在开头段落前先自测）

```text
My research vision is <one sentence>.
In the next 3–5 years, I will build <2–3 thrusts>, enabled by <methods/resources>,
to deliver <specific outcomes> and train students in <skills>.
```

## Thrust 段落结构示例（标题即可）

```text
Thrust 1: <name>
1) Problem & significance
2) Approach / methods
3) Evidence from prior work
4) Milestones (1/3/5 years)
5) Risks & alternatives
```
