# Examples

## 内部评审请求邮件模板（可改写）

```text
Subject: Red-team review request (NSF-style proposal, due YYYY-MM-DD)

Could you review (1) the Project Summary and (2) the section headings + 1-page outline?
What I need most:
- Is the story clear to a non-specialist?
- Any obvious feasibility or risk gaps?
- Any missing components from a reviewer’s perspective?

Thank you!
```
